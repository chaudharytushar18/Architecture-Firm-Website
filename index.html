About Our Studio

At Aakaar Studio, we believe architecture is more than buildings — it is about crafting environments that enrich everyday life. Our portfolio spans residential towers, commercial hubs, institutional campuses, hospitality venues, urban design, exhibition spaces, interiors, and landscape planning across India.

Led by principals Ananya Sharma and Rohit Malhotra, the studio brings together architects, interior designers, civil engineers, and site coordinators. Supported by consultants in MEP, structure, and sustainability, we deliver context-sensitive design rooted in climate, culture, and community needs.

Every project reflects our focus on innovation and client collaboration — from compact urban homes to large hospital complexes. Explore our work to see how ideas become enduring built environments.

Our Philosophy

  • Innovation: We explore new materials and planning strategies to create spaces that are functional, inspiring, and future-ready.
  • Sustainability: Passive cooling, daylight optimisation, rainwater harvesting, and low-carbon materials guide our design decisions.
  • Context: We respect local heritage, neighbourhood scale, and regional climate so architecture feels rooted, not imported.

Principal Architects

Ananya Sharma

Ananya co-founded Aakaar Studio after eighteen years leading healthcare and institutional projects. An alumna of SPA Delhi, she specialises in patient-centric planning, campus master planning, and design leadership across North India.

Rohit Malhotra

Rohit directs hospitality and cultural projects at the studio. Trained at CEPT Ahmedabad, he blends contemporary form-making with crafts-led interiors. His work includes boutique hotels, restaurant chains, and adaptive reuse of heritage structures.
